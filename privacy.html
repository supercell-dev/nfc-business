Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Data Collection

We only collect the information necessary to fulfill your order. For **Elite Cards**, this includes the name and logo you provide for customization. Contact information is only used for delivery purposes within Casablanca and Morocco.

2. NFC Data Security

SuperTap hardware acts as a bridge to your digital profile. We do not store your passwords or private login credentials. The data written to your NFC card or tag is controlled by you via your chosen profile provider.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is strictly used for order processing and customer support.

4. Local Compliance

As a Casablanca-based provider, we respect local privacy standards and strive to ensure your digital networking experience is secure and professional.

If you have questions about your data, contact us via WhatsApp or our official TikTok channel.
