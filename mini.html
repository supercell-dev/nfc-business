Mini Series FAQ

Do they work with all phones?

Yes! They work with almost all modern iPhones (7 and newer) and most Android devices. For older phones, use the QR backup on your profile.

Where should I stick my Mini Tag?

For iPhones, place the tag at the **bottom** of the phone. For Androids, the center or bottom is best. Avoid covering the internal NFC sensor.

Can I change the link later?

Absolutely. Your tag links to your digital profile. You can update your social links or website anytime without needing a new tag.
